PHASE 3 DNA ISOLATION

Extract the DNA from the cells found on the pizza crust.

Cells

1. Lysis Buffer (Detergent)

Detergent breaks open the cell membrane and nuclear envelope to release the DNA.

2. Protease (Enzyme)

Protease enzymes cut apart the histone proteins that the DNA is tightly wrapped around.

3. Ice-Cold Alcohol

DNA is not soluble in alcohol. Adding it causes the DNA to precipitate (clump together) so we can see it.

PHASE 4 AMPLIFICATION & DIGESTION

1. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction)
